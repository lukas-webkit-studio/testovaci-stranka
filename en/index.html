Guest House in the heart of the Rock Town

Stay in the heart of the Rock Town
in „Adršpašsko – Teplické skály“

Enjoy the moments that you can spend wandering with your family in beatiful nature. They are the most valuable. Create wonderful memories not only of natural wonders but also of cosy accommodation.

The rooms for 4 persons with their own facilities will provide privacy but in the same time you can enjoy summer terrace and common areas with your friends.

We are available all year long. You can explore the magic of all four seasons.
Fresh air and absolute peace will guarantee sleep which you will remember for a long time.
Our Guest House is situated right on doorstep of the Rock Town „Adršpašsko – Teplické skály“. You can choose from several tourist trails.
This region is ideal for small cyclist as well as experienced athletes.
On our summer terrace you can barbecue and have fun with your friends.
